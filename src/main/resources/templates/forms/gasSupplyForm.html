Gas supply

Include any gas supply, even if it's currently unused.

Gas appliances

Gas appliances include any:

  • gas boilers (for heating or hot water)
  • gas cookers or hobs
  • gas fires or heaters
  • gas water heaters
  • other fixed or portable gas appliances

Do not include any portable gas appliances that use detachable gas bottles. For example, a barbecue or patio heater.
