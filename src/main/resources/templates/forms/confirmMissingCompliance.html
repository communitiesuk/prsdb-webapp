This property is occupied by tenants, but it’s missing compliance certificates.

Missing gas safety certificate

A valid gas safety certificate shows that a Gas Safe engineer has checked the gas supply and appliances within the last 12 months.

Without this certificate, you can be:

  • fined an unlimited amount
  • sent to prison for up to 6 months

Missing electrical safety certificate

A valid electrical safety certificate shows that an electrician has checked the electrics in the last 5 years.

Without this certificate, you can be:

  • fined or prosecuted
  • ordered by the local council to get an inspection done

Missing EPC

A valid EPC shows that the property meets the minimum energy efficiency standards for rental properties.

Without this certificate, you can be:

  • fined or prosecuted
  • unable to use certain grounds for possession to evict tenants
