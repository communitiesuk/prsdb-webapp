Private Rented Sector Database

This service holds information about private landlords and their rented properties. Private landlords in England are legally required to use this service.

Who can access this service

If you are a landlord:

  • you are legally required to register with this service and give up to date information about your properties

If you manage properties on behalf of a landlord:

  • you can get access to this service through your letting agent
  • a landlord can assign you properties to manage

Access the Private Rented Sector Database

You'll be asked to sign in using GOV UK One Login and then you can sign in or register for the service.

Your responsibility to register

Landlords who let under an assured or regulated tenancy in England must register for this service.

Understand your responsibility to register in the Guide to the Renters' Rights Bill .

When registering, you must provide accurate and up to date information about:

  • yourself
  • a landlord can assign you properties to manage
  • joint owners or interested parties

You'll be asked to pay a specific amount for each property your register.

You should renew your details every 3 years.

How to register

You can register:

  • online using this page
  • by telephone
  • using a paper form

Register by phone

If you need additional help to register, you contact the helpline centres by calling:

  • Telephone: 0000 000 000
  • Telephone: +44 00 0000 0000 (if calling from outside the UK)

You can also contact the helpline and ask for a form.

Register by email or post

You can download an application form to register as a landlord .

You can email the completed form to landlordregistration@uk.gov.uk .
